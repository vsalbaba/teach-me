# Mission: Product Requirements Documents (PRDs)

## Why
Write actionable PRDs that translate product thinking into clear, implementable requirements. As a developer and QE engineer with product management interest, bridge the gap between "what to build" and "how to break it into work."

## Success looks like
- Can write a PRD from scratch that a development team can act on
- Can evaluate and improve an existing PRD for clarity and completeness
- Can decompose a PRD into trackable issues (stories, tasks, bugs)

## Constraints
- Format: Markdown, stored locally (no Confluence or SaaS tools)
- Background: Developer and QE -- technical depth is a strength, product framing is the growth area

## Out of scope
- Product strategy and roadmapping (broader PM discipline)
- Specific project management tool workflows (Jira mechanics, etc.)
- User research methodology
